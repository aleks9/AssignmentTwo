Style Guide

Visually appealing - The site must have attractive mix of text, photos and graphics and should give customers a strong impression of the hotel service, features and characteristics.

Theme of the site - warm tones, giving the feeling of home, highlights, simple and clear language, images reflect the theme of the site, and showing the site's personality and taste. Promotion and corporate logo will through the site to enhance their visibility.

For ease of use, the website must provide consistent orientation and navigational aids, such as hierarchical menus that tell users how deep they are into a topic or subtopic as well as a homepage link or icon on each page in the same position.
